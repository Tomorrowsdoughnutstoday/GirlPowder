GirlPowder is a snowboarding club for women. Whatever your ability, style or goal, get to the next level by joining a super cool snowboarding crew. We’re an easygoing bunch and often our meetups will involve a leasuirely day riding together or an afternoon spent at the park. And every-so-often, we’ll be joined by a special guest who might just teach us something new!
